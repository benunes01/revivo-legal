Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Revivo ("we", "our", or "us"), operated by Be Captain Studio, provides the Revivo mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign in, we collect your authentication credentials through Firebase Authentication (Google, Apple, or anonymous sign-in). This may include your name, email address, and profile picture.

Photos: When you use our restoration service, we temporarily process the photos you upload. Photos are stored securely on our servers for the sole purpose of performing AI restoration.

Usage Data: We collect anonymous analytics data through Firebase Analytics, including app usage patterns, feature interactions, and crash reports to improve our service.

Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and network information to ensure compatibility and troubleshoot issues.

Purchase Information: Subscription and purchase data is managed by RevenueCat and the respective app stores (Apple App Store, Google Play Store). We do not store your payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our photo restoration service
  • To process your uploaded photos using AI models
  • To manage your account and subscription
  • To track your restoration history and credits
  • To send push notifications about completed restorations (if enabled)
  • To improve our app through anonymous analytics
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse of our services

3. Photo Processing & Storage

Your photos are uploaded to secure cloud storage (Amazon S3) for processing. Original and restored photos are retained in your account for your access. You can delete your restoration history at any time. When you delete your account, all associated photos are permanently removed within 30 days.

We use third-party AI models (via Replicate) to process your photos. Photos sent to these services are used solely for restoration and are not retained by the AI providers after processing is complete.

Important: We do not use your photos for training AI models, advertising, or any purpose other than providing the restoration service you requested.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We share data only with the following service providers, strictly for operating our service:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, analytics, and push notifications
  • Amazon Web Services: Secure photo storage and content delivery
  • Replicate: AI photo processing (photos are processed and not retained)
  • RevenueCat: Subscription and purchase management

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and the safety of our users.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure token-based authentication with automatic expiration
  • Access-controlled cloud storage with presigned URLs
  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse
  • Regular security reviews of our infrastructure

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in our systems
  • Delete your account and all associated data (via Settings in the app)
  • Export your restored photos to your device
  • Opt out of push notifications (via device settings)
  • Opt out of analytics data collection (by contacting us)
  • Rectify inaccurate personal data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at admin@becaptainstudio.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete such information.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Account data: As long as your account is active
  • Photos and restorations: Until you delete them or delete your account
  • Analytics data: Retained in anonymous, aggregate form
  • After account deletion: All personal data is permanently removed within 30 days

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored on servers located outside your country of residence, including in the United States. By using our service, you consent to the transfer of your data to these locations. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this policy.

10. LGPD Compliance (Brazil)

For users in Brazil, we comply with the Lei Geral de Prote\u00e7\u00e3o de Dados (LGPD). You have the right to request confirmation of data processing, access, correction, anonymization, portability, and deletion of your personal data. Contact our Data Protection Officer at admin@becaptainstudio.com.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
