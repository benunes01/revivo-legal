Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Revivo ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Revivo mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign in, we collect your authentication credentials through Firebase Authentication (Google, Apple, or anonymous sign-in). This may include your name, email address, and profile picture.

Photos: When you use our restoration service, we temporarily process the photos you upload. Photos are stored securely on our servers for the sole purpose of performing AI restoration.

Usage Data: We collect anonymous analytics data through Firebase Analytics, including app usage patterns, feature interactions, and crash reports to improve our service.

Purchase Information: Subscription and purchase data is managed by RevenueCat and the respective app stores (Apple App Store, Google Play Store). We do not store your payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our photo restoration service
  • To process your uploaded photos using AI models
  • To manage your account and subscription
  • To track your restoration history and credits
  • To send push notifications about completed restorations (if enabled)
  • To improve our app through anonymous analytics

3. Photo Processing & Storage

Your photos are uploaded to secure cloud storage (Amazon S3) for processing. Original and restored photos are retained in your account for your access. You can delete your restoration history at any time. When you delete your account, all associated photos are permanently removed.

We use third-party AI models (via Replicate) to process your photos. Photos sent to these services are used solely for restoration and are not retained by the AI providers after processing is complete.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We share data only with:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and analytics
  • Amazon Web Services: Secure photo storage
  • Replicate: AI photo processing
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), secure token-based authentication, and access-controlled cloud storage. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your restored photos
  • Opt out of push notifications
  • Opt out of analytics (by contacting us)

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

8. Data Retention

We retain your account data and restoration history as long as your account is active. Photos are retained until you delete them or delete your account. Anonymous analytics data may be retained indefinitely in aggregate form.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in servers located outside your country of residence. By using our service, you consent to the transfer of your data to these locations.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
