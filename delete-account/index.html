Delete Your Account

Revivo — AI Photo Restoration by Be Captain Studio

You can delete your Revivo account and all associated data at any time. Here's how:

How to Delete Your Account

1 Open Revivo

Launch the Revivo app on your device.

2 Go to Settings

Tap the Settings tab in the bottom navigation bar.

3 Tap "Manage Account"

In the Profile section at the top, tap Manage account.

4 Confirm Deletion

A confirmation dialog will appear. Tap Delete to permanently delete your account. This action cannot be undone.

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed within 30 days:

Data Type Action Timeframe
Account profile (name, email) DELETED Immediately
Uploaded photos (originals) DELETED Within 30 days
Restored photos DELETED Within 30 days
Restoration history DELETED Immediately
Credit balance DELETED Immediately
Push notification token DELETED Immediately
Subscription status DELETED Immediately
Anonymous analytics data RETAINED Aggregate, non-identifiable
Purchase receipts RETAINED Managed by App Store / Play Store

Note: If you have an active subscription, deleting your account does not automatically cancel it. Please cancel your subscription through your device's account settings (App Store or Google Play) before deleting your Revivo account to avoid future charges.

Can't Access the App?

If you are unable to access the app to delete your account, you can request account deletion by email. Please include the email address associated with your account.

Send your deletion request to:

admin@becaptainstudio.com

We will process your request within 30 days.
